Games We Play

1. MIR4

MIR4 is set in a vast open world with a focus on martial arts and features a player-driven economy. The game offers a dynamic combat system, allowing players to engage in both player versus environment (PvE) and player versus player (PvP) battles. Players can explore the game world, complete quests, train in various martial arts, and participate in large-scale battles.

2. Black Desert Online

Black Desert Online is known for its impressive graphics, seamless open-world exploration, and dynamic combat system. Set in a high-fantasy world, the game offers players the freedom to create unique characters with a detailed character creation system. Players can choose from various classes, each with its own distinct playstyle and abilities.

3. Final Fantasy 7 Remake

"Final Fantasy VII Remake" takes players back to the dystopian world of Midgar, where the eco-terrorist group AVALANCHE, led by the protagonist Cloud Strife, opposes the powerful Shinra Electric Power Company. The game features a real-time combat system with a mix of action and strategic elements, departing from the turn-based system of the original game. Players control Cloud and other party members, each with their unique abilities, as they explore the city, battle enemies, and unravel a complex narrative filled with political intrigue and personal struggles.

4. PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds

PUBG is a multiplayer online battle royale game that pits up to 100 players against each other on a large open map. The goal is to be the last person or team standing by eliminating other players. Players start with no equipment and must scavenge for weapons, armor, and supplies while avoiding a shrinking play zone that forces encounters between participants. The game combines elements of survival, exploration, and intense combat, and it offers both solo and team-based gameplay.

5. Apex Legends

Apex Legends is set in the Titanfall universe and stands out among other battle royale games for its focus on team-based gameplay and unique character abilities. In the game, players form squads of three and choose from a diverse cast of "Legends," each with their own distinct personalities, backstories, and special abilities. These abilities include offensive, defensive, and support skills, adding a layer of strategy to team dynamics.

6. Farlight 84

Farlight 84 on Steam. Fast-Paced Hero shooter Battle Royale. Multi-platform interoperable. With flexible jetpacks, diverse hero skills, armed vehicles, a special in-game growth system and multiple revives, Farlight 84 brings you quicker battles with more compact mayhem and rampage!

7. Call of Duty: Warzone

Call of Duty: Warzone is part of the Call of Duty franchise and serves as its standalone battle royale experience. It was released in March 2020. The game is set in the fictional city of Verdansk, featuring a vast open world with various environments, including urban areas, suburbs, and industrial zones. Up to 150 players, either solo or in teams, parachute onto the map and engage in a last-person-standing competition.

8. VALORANT

VALORANT blends traditional FPS gameplay with unique character-based abilities. Players assume the roles of "Agents," each with distinctive abilities and playstyles. The game features a 5v5 format where one team plays as attackers trying to plant a bomb called the Spike, while the other team defends. The matches consist of multiple rounds, with teams switching between attacking and defending.

9. Counter-Strike 2

A free upgrade to CS:GO, Counter-Strike 2 marks the largest technical leap in Counter-Strike's history. Built on the Source 2 engine, Counter-Strike 2 is modernized with realistic physically-based rendering, state of the art networking, and upgraded Community Workshop tools.

10. Overwatch 2

Overwatch 2 is the sequel to the highly successful and team-based multiplayer shooter, "Overwatch." The game retains the team-based, objective-oriented gameplay that made the original popular while introducing several new features. One significant addition is the inclusion of a story-driven campaign mode that allows players to experience a narrative-driven cooperative experience.

11. Titanfall 2

Set in a sci-fi universe where players take on the role of Pilots, elite soldiers equipped with powerful mech-style exoskeletons known as Titans, Titanfall 2 offers a unique blend of fast-paced infantry combat and intense mech battles. The game features a single-player campaign that follows the story of Jack Cooper, a Rifleman who bonds with a Titan named BT-7274. Together, they navigate through various missions and face challenges from both hostile forces and hostile environments.

12. Destiny 2

Destiny 2 is set in a mythic science fiction world where players, known as Guardians, assume the role of powerful defenders of humanity. The game features a combination of first-person shooter gameplay and elements of massively multiplayer online games (MMOs).

13. Halo: Combat Evolved

Set in a futuristic science fiction universe, Halo: Combat Evolved follows the story of Master Chief, a supersoldier equipped with advanced armor known as MJOLNIR, and his artificial intelligence companion Cortana. The game begins as the UNSC (United Nations Space Command) ship Pillar of Autumn escapes an enemy alien race known as the Covenant. The ship discovers a mysterious ringworld structure called Halo, leading to a series of intense battles against both the Covenant forces and a parasitic alien species known as the Flood.

14. Left 4 Dead 2

Set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by a zombie outbreak, Left 4 Dead 2 follows the journey of four survivors as they attempt to navigate through various environments, fight hordes of infected, and reach safety. The game is designed for cooperative gameplay, allowing up to four players to join forces online or locally to battle the zombie horde.

15. Far Cry

Set in the fictional Caribbean island nation of Yara, Far Cry 6 introduces players to a politically charged and volatile environment. Players assume the role of Dani Rojas, a guerrilla fighter seeking to overthrow the oppressive regime of Antón Castillo, played by Giancarlo Esposito. Antón is the dictator of Yara and is grooming his son, Diego, to succeed him.

16. Minecraft

Minecraft is a game that allows players to explore a blocky, procedurally-generated 3D world with infinite terrain, discovering and extracting raw materials, crafting tools and items, and building structures or earthworks. Depending on the game mode, players can also engage in combat with computer-controlled "mobs" and cooperate with or compete against other players in the same world.
